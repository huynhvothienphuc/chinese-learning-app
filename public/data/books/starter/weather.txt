晴天 | qíngtiān (sunny) | 今天是晴天，我們去公園吧。 | Jīntiān shì qíngtiān, wǒmen qù gōngyuán ba. | It is sunny today, so let's go to the park.
下雨 | xiàyǔ (rainy) | 外面下雨了，記得帶傘。 | Wàimiàn xiàyǔ le, jìde dài sǎn. | It is raining outside, remember to bring an umbrella.
多雲 | duōyún (cloudy) | 下午可能會多雲。 | Xiàwǔ kěnéng huì duōyún. | It may be cloudy in the afternoon.
陰天 | yīntiān (overcast) | 陰天的時候很適合散步。 | Yīntiān de shíhou hěn shìhé sànbù. | Overcast weather is good for walking.
颳風 | guāfēng (windy) | 今天颳風很大。 | Jīntiān guāfēng hěn dà. | It is very windy today.
很熱 | hěn rè (hot) | 夏天中午常常很熱。 | Xiàtiān zhōngwǔ chángcháng hěn rè. | Summer noons are often very hot.
很冷 | hěn lěng (cold) | 冬天早上很冷。 | Dōngtiān zǎoshang hěn lěng. | Winter mornings are very cold.
下雪 | xiàxuě (snowy) | 山上已經開始下雪了。 | Shān shàng yǐjīng kāishǐ xiàxuě le. | It has started snowing in the mountains.
溫暖 | wēnnuǎn (warm) | 春天的陽光很溫暖。 | Chūntiān de yángguāng hěn wēnnuǎn. | The spring sunshine is warm.
涼快 | liángkuai (cool) | 晚上的風很涼快。 | Wǎnshàng de fēng hěn liángkuai. | The wind at night is cool.

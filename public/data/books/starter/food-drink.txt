米飯 | mǐfàn (rice) | 我晚餐想吃米飯。 | Wǒ wǎncān xiǎng chī mǐfàn. | I want to eat rice for dinner.
麵 | miàn (noodles) | 這家店的麵很好吃。 | Zhè jiā diàn de miàn hěn hǎochī. | The noodles at this shop are delicious.
水 | shuǐ (water) | 運動以後要多喝水。 | Yùndòng yǐhòu yào duō hē shuǐ. | Drink more water after exercise.
茶 | chá (tea) | 爺爺下午喜歡喝茶。 | Yéye xiàwǔ xǐhuān hē chá. | Grandpa likes to drink tea in the afternoon.
咖啡 | kāfēi (coffee) | 我早上喝一杯咖啡。 | Wǒ zǎoshang hē yì bēi kāfēi. | I drink a cup of coffee in the morning.
蘋果 | píngguǒ (apple) | 她每天帶一顆蘋果去學校。 | Tā měitiān dài yì kē píngguǒ qù xuéxiào. | She brings an apple to school every day.
牛奶 | niúnǎi (milk) | 小朋友早餐喝牛奶。 | Xiǎopéngyǒu zǎocān hē niúnǎi. | Children drink milk for breakfast.
餃子 | jiǎozi (dumplings) | 我們週末常常一起包餃子。 | Wǒmen zhōumò chángcháng yìqǐ bāo jiǎozi. | We often make dumplings together on weekends.

爸爸 | bàba (father) | 我爸爸會修腳踏車。 | Wǒ bàba huì xiū jiǎotàchē. | My father can repair bicycles.
媽媽 | māma (mother) | 我媽媽正在做晚飯。 | Wǒ māma zhèngzài zuò wǎnfàn. | My mother is cooking dinner.
哥哥 | gēge (older brother) | 我哥哥每天跑步。 | Wǒ gēge měitiān pǎobù. | My older brother runs every day.
姐姐 | jiějie (older sister) | 姐姐教我畫畫。 | Jiějie jiāo wǒ huàhuà. | My older sister teaches me to draw.
弟弟 | dìdi (younger brother) | 弟弟喜歡玩積木。 | Dìdi xǐhuān wán jīmù. | My younger brother likes playing with blocks.
妹妹 | mèimei (younger sister) | 妹妹在房間裡唱歌。 | Mèimei zài fángjiān lǐ chànggē. | My younger sister is singing in the room.
爺爺 | yéye (grandfather) | 爺爺早上去散步。 | Yéye zǎoshang qù sànbù. | Grandpa goes for a walk in the morning.
奶奶 | nǎinai (grandmother) | 奶奶會做很好吃的湯。 | Nǎinai huì zuò hěn hǎochī de tāng. | Grandma makes very tasty soup.
兒子 | érzi (son) | 他兒子今年上小學。 | Tā érzi jīnnián shàng xiǎoxué. | His son started elementary school this year.
女兒 | nǚ'ér (daughter) | 她女兒喜歡看故事書。 | Tā nǚ'ér xǐhuān kàn gùshìshū. | Her daughter likes reading storybooks.

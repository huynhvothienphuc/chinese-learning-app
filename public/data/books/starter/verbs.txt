吃 | chī (eat) | 我喜歡吃水果。 | Wǒ xǐhuān chī shuǐguǒ. | I like to eat fruit.
喝 | hē (drink) | 他每天喝很多水。 | Tā měitiān hē hěn duō shuǐ. | He drinks a lot of water every day.
看 | kàn (look) | 請看黑板。 | Qǐng kàn hēibǎn. | Please look at the blackboard.
聽 | tīng (listen) | 我喜歡聽音樂。 | Wǒ xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè. | I like listening to music.
說 | shuō (speak) | 她會說中文和英文。 | Tā huì shuō Zhōngwén hé Yīngwén. | She can speak Chinese and English.
寫 | xiě (write) | 我正在寫日記。 | Wǒ zhèngzài xiě rìjì. | I am writing a diary.
去 | qù (go) | 我們現在去學校。 | Wǒmen xiànzài qù xuéxiào. | We are going to school now.
來 | lái (come) | 你明天可以來我家嗎？ | Nǐ míngtiān kěyǐ lái wǒ jiā ma? | Can you come to my house tomorrow?
走 | zǒu (walk) | 我每天走路去公園。 | Wǒ měitiān zǒulù qù gōngyuán. | I walk to the park every day.
跑 | pǎo (run) | 小狗在院子裡跑。 | Xiǎogǒu zài yuànzi lǐ pǎo. | The puppy runs in the yard.
睡覺 | shuìjiào (sleep) | 寶寶晚上九點睡覺。 | Bǎobǎo wǎnshàng jiǔ diǎn shuìjiào. | The baby sleeps at nine at night.
讀 | dú (read) | 他喜歡讀小說。 | Tā xǐhuān dú xiǎoshuō. | He likes reading novels.

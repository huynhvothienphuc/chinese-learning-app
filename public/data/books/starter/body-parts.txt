頭 | tóu (head) | 他點了一下頭。 | Tā diǎnle yíxià tóu. | He nodded his head.
眼睛 | yǎnjīng (eyes) | 她的眼睛很明亮。 | Tā de yǎnjīng hěn míngliàng. | Her eyes are bright.
耳朵 | ěrduo (ears) | 小兔子的耳朵很長。 | Xiǎo tùzi de ěrduo hěn cháng. | A rabbit's ears are long.
鼻子 | bízi (nose) | 我的鼻子有點不舒服。 | Wǒ de bízi yǒudiǎn bù shūfu. | My nose feels a little uncomfortable.
嘴巴 | zuǐba (mouth) | 請張開嘴巴。 | Qǐng zhāngkāi zuǐba. | Please open your mouth.
手 | shǒu (hand) | 洗手以前要先關水。 | Xǐ shǒu yǐqián yào xiān guān shuǐ. | Turn off the water before washing your hands.
腳 | jiǎo (foot) | 他的腳受傷了。 | Tā de jiǎo shòushāng le. | His foot is injured.
肩膀 | jiānbǎng (shoulder) | 書包掛在我的肩膀上。 | Shūbāo guà zài wǒ de jiānbǎng shàng. | The backpack hangs on my shoulder.
肚子 | dùzi (belly) | 我吃太多了，肚子很飽。 | Wǒ chī tài duō le, dùzi hěn bǎo. | I ate too much, and my belly is full.
頭髮 | tóufa (hair) | 她把頭髮綁起來了。 | Tā bǎ tóufa bǎng qǐlái le. | She tied up her hair.

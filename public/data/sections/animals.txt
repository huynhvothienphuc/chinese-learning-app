狗 | gǒu (dog) | 那隻狗很喜歡玩球。 | Nà zhī gǒu hěn xǐhuān wán qiú. | That dog loves playing with a ball.
貓 | māo (cat) | 我家的貓正在睡覺。 | Wǒ jiā de māo zhèngzài shuìjiào. | My cat is sleeping.
鳥 | niǎo (bird) | 窗外有一隻小鳥。 | Chuāngwài yǒu yì zhī xiǎoniǎo. | There is a little bird outside the window.
魚 | yú (fish) | 水族箱裡有很多魚。 | Shuǐzúxiāng lǐ yǒu hěn duō yú. | There are many fish in the aquarium.
兔子 | tùzi (rabbit) | 兔子喜歡吃紅蘿蔔。 | Tùzi xǐhuān chī hóngluóbo. | Rabbits like eating carrots.
馬 | mǎ (horse) | 那匹馬跑得很快。 | Nà pǐ mǎ pǎo de hěn kuài. | That horse runs very fast.
牛 | niú (cow) | 農場裡有幾頭牛。 | Nóngchǎng lǐ yǒu jǐ tóu niú. | There are several cows on the farm.
羊 | yáng (sheep) | 小羊在草地上吃草。 | Xiǎo yáng zài cǎodì shàng chī cǎo. | The lamb is eating grass on the field.
老虎 | lǎohǔ (tiger) | 老虎住在森林裡。 | Lǎohǔ zhù zài sēnlín lǐ. | Tigers live in the forest.
熊貓 | xióngmāo (panda) | 熊貓最喜歡吃竹子。 | Xióngmāo zuì xǐhuān chī zhúzi. | Pandas like bamboo best.
猴子 | hóuzi (monkey) | 猴子爬到樹上去了。 | Hóuzi pá dào shù shàng qù le. | The monkey climbed up the tree.
大象 | dàxiàng (elephant) | 大象的鼻子很長。 | Dàxiàng de bízi hěn cháng. | An elephant's trunk is long.

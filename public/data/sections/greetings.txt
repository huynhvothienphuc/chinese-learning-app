你好 | nǐ hǎo (hello) | 看到老師時要說你好。 | Kàndào lǎoshī shí yào shuō nǐ hǎo. | Say hello when you see the teacher.
早安 | zǎo'ān (good morning) | 我每天都跟媽媽說早安。 | Wǒ měitiān dōu gēn māma shuō zǎo'ān. | I say good morning to my mom every day.
午安 | wǔ'ān (good afternoon) | 午安，今天過得怎麼樣？ | Wǔ'ān, jīntiān guò de zěnmeyàng? | Good afternoon, how is your day going?
晚安 | wǎn'ān (good night) | 睡前別忘了說晚安。 | Shuì qián bié wàngle shuō wǎn'ān. | Don't forget to say good night before sleeping.
謝謝 | xièxie (thank you) | 別人幫忙時要說謝謝。 | Biérén bāngmáng shí yào shuō xièxie. | Say thank you when someone helps you.
不客氣 | bú kèqi (you're welcome) | 別人說謝謝時，你可以說不客氣。 | Biérén shuō xièxie shí, nǐ kěyǐ shuō bú kèqi. | When someone says thank you, you can say you're welcome.
再見 | zàijiàn (goodbye) | 下課後我們跟同學說再見。 | Xiàkè hòu wǒmen gēn tóngxué shuō zàijiàn. | We say goodbye to classmates after class.

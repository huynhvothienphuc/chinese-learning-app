星期一 | xīngqīyī (Monday) | 星期一我有中文課。 | Xīngqīyī wǒ yǒu Zhōngwén kè. | I have Chinese class on Monday.
星期二 | xīngqī'èr (Tuesday) | 星期二我們去圖書館。 | Xīngqī'èr wǒmen qù túshūguǎn. | We go to the library on Tuesday.
星期三 | xīngqīsān (Wednesday) | 星期三下午他要開會。 | Xīngqīsān xiàwǔ tā yào kāihuì. | He has a meeting on Wednesday afternoon.
星期四 | xīngqīsì (Thursday) | 星期四晚上我做作業。 | Xīngqīsì wǎnshàng wǒ zuò zuòyè. | I do homework on Thursday evening.
星期五 | xīngqīwǔ (Friday) | 星期五大家都很開心。 | Xīngqīwǔ dàjiā dōu hěn kāixīn. | Everyone is happy on Friday.
星期六 | xīngqīliù (Saturday) | 星期六我們去看電影。 | Xīngqīliù wǒmen qù kàn diànyǐng. | We go to the movies on Saturday.
星期日 | xīngqīrì (Sunday) | 星期日我在家休息。 | Xīngqīrì wǒ zài jiā xiūxi. | I rest at home on Sunday.
今天 | jīntiān (today) | 今天的天氣很舒服。 | Jīntiān de tiānqì hěn shūfu. | The weather is pleasant today.
明天 | míngtiān (tomorrow) | 明天我們要考試。 | Míngtiān wǒmen yào kǎoshì. | We have an exam tomorrow.
昨天 | zuótiān (yesterday) | 昨天我看了一本新書。 | Zuótiān wǒ kànle yì běn xīn shū. | I read a new book yesterday.

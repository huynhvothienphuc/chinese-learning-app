一 | yī (one) | 我今天買了一個麵包。 | Wǒ jīntiān mǎile yī ge miànbāo. | I bought one bread today.
二 | èr (two) | 桌上有兩本書。 | Zhuō shàng yǒu liǎng běn shū. | There are two books on the table.
三 | sān (three) | 我們家有三個人。 | Wǒmen jiā yǒu sān ge rén. | There are three people in our family.
四 | sì (four) | 教室裡有四扇窗戶。 | Jiàoshì lǐ yǒu sì shàn chuānghù. | There are four windows in the classroom.
五 | wǔ (five) | 她買了五支筆。 | Tā mǎile wǔ zhī bǐ. | She bought five pens.
六 | liù (six) | 我六點起床。 | Wǒ liù diǎn qǐchuáng. | I get up at six o'clock.
七 | qī (seven) | 一週有七天。 | Yì zhōu yǒu qī tiān. | There are seven days in a week.
八 | bā (eight) | 他八歲開始學鋼琴。 | Tā bā suì kāishǐ xué gāngqín. | He started learning piano at eight years old.
九 | jiǔ (nine) | 我們九月開學。 | Wǒmen jiǔ yuè kāixué. | We start school in September.
十 | shí (ten) | 這裡有十張椅子。 | Zhèlǐ yǒu shí zhāng yǐzi. | There are ten chairs here.
十一 | shíyī (eleven) | 現在是十一點。 | Xiànzài shì shíyī diǎn. | It is eleven o'clock now.
十二 | shí'èr (twelve) | 一年有十二個月。 | Yì nián yǒu shí'èr ge yuè. | There are twelve months in a year.
十三 | shísān (thirteen) | 他十三歲就會做飯。 | Tā shísān suì jiù huì zuò fàn. | He could cook at thirteen.
十四 | shísì (fourteen) | 我們班有十四個學生。 | Wǒmen bān yǒu shísì ge xuéshēng. | Our class has fourteen students.
十五 | shíwǔ (fifteen) | 公車十五分鐘後到。 | Gōngchē shíwǔ fēnzhōng hòu dào. | The bus arrives in fifteen minutes.
十六 | shíliù (sixteen) | 她十六歲學會游泳。 | Tā shíliù suì xuéhuì yóuyǒng. | She learned to swim at sixteen.
十七 | shíqī (seventeen) | 他住在十七樓。 | Túshūguǎn shíqī hào guānmén. | The library closes on the seventeenth.
十八 | shíbā (eighteen) | 他今年十八歲。 | Tā jīnnián shíbā suì. | He is eighteen this year.
十九 | shíjiǔ (nineteen) | 我們班有十九位學生。 | Huǒchē zài shíjiǔ fēnzhōng hòu chūfā. | The train leaves in nineteen minutes.
二十 | èrshí (twenty) | 這本書二十元。 | Zhè běn shū èrshí yuán. | This book costs twenty yuan.
